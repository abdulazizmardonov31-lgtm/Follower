About Follower

Solar panels produce the most electricity when they face the sun directly — but fixed panels often miss optimal angles during the day. Follower is an intelligent, low-cost solar tracking system that automatically follows the sun's position using light-dependent resistors (LDRs) and servo motors to maximize sunlight exposure and increase energy output.

Use cases

Residential rooftop panels, agricultural irrigation systems, remote/off-grid stations, educational projects and prototypes. Easy to adapt for different panel sizes and environments.

How it works

Detect

Multiple LDR sensors compare light intensity from different directions to determine where the sun is brightest.

Rotate

Microcontroller sends commands to two servo motors that adjust the panel horizontally and vertically.

Optimize

The control loop continuously corrects the panel angle so it stays aligned across the day.
